Free treatments are available. The hospital is equipped with 250 beds for the admitted patients. There is also a clubfoot treatment facility here, for the children, called the Walk for Life. The hospital has a medical waste management procedure where a yellow van collects the waste on regular basis. The premise is fairly clean and the buildings are well painted. The hospital has an ancient one-storied building that was built by Kalipada Ghosh in memory of his father a Zaminder called Babu Bholanath Ghosh.
